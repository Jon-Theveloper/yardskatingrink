OUR STORY

Yard Skating Rink was founded in 2015 by a group of skating enthusiasts who wanted to create a unique experience that combined retro roller rink vibes with modern neon aesthetics.

What started as a small local rink has grown into one of the most popular skating destinations in the region, known for our vibrant atmosphere and commitment to the skating community.
